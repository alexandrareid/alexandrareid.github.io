My Cat, Tabitha

I got my cat Tabitha for my 14th birthday. We went to the shelter to pick her out and there she was, a little ball of fuzz and I knew I had to have her!

We brought her home, but she didn't have a name for a few weeks so we just called her all sorts of things and my grandma still to this day calls her "baby kitty"

This is one of my favorite pictures I took of Tabitha, she is just so precious!

Tabitha is about 5 years old and is a grey Tabby cat rescue. When we first got her, her name was spooky boo

I have had Tabby for about 5 years, but over the summer she ran away and we haven't seen her since, I miss her a lot and hope one day she returns
